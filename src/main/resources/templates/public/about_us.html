About EveryDay Help

Our certified electricians are available for installations, repairs, and maintenance. Whether it's fixing wiring or installing new fixtures, safety and reliability are our priorities.

Gardener

Our experienced gardeners help you maintain lush green lawns, healthy plants, and clean outdoor spaces. Services include pruning, planting, mowing, and seasonal care.

Trained domestic helpers available for daily cleaning, dishwashing, dusting, laundry, and other household chores to maintain a clean and organized home.

Expert plumbers for fixing leaks, unclogging drains, pipe fittings, bathroom repairs, and water tank services. Fast, clean, and efficient plumbing solutions.

Painter

Brighten your space with our professional painting services. We offer wall prep, indoor/outdoor painting, and texture work using quality tools and finishes.

Cook

Hire home cooks who can prepare daily meals, follow special diets, or cook for events. Our cooks are experienced with Indian and regional cuisines.

Certified welders for gate repair, railing installations, fabrication, and metal work. Precision and strength are guaranteed with our welding services.

Locksmith

Lost keys? Broken locks? Our locksmiths offer fast and secure solutions for home, office, and vehicle locks, including installations and repairs.
